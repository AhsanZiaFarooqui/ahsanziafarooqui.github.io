I am a PhD candidate in Economics at the University of Sussex and a Doctoral Fellow at the International Centre for Tax and Development (ICTD).

My research interests lie in public finance and state capacity, with a focus on how tax systems and frontline bureaucrats shape outcomes in Pakistan. In recent years, I have focused on subnational taxation, bureaucratic discretion, and the design of digital public infrastructure in property and excise taxation.

I work closely with government partners through IDEAS on teacher recruitment, merit-based reforms, and education policy.
