About

I am a first-year PhD Economics student at the University of Sussex and a Doctoral Fellow at the International Centre for Tax and Development (ICTD). My research focuses on the drivers of state capacity and how citizens interact with the state, with a particular emphasis on policing, tax, and other core bureaucracies in Pakistan.

Since 2013, I have been working closely with public sector institutions on questions of tax administration, public finance, and governance. I also serve as Senior Programs Manager and Consulting Lead at the Institute of Development and Economic Alternatives (IDEAS) .

My research fields are public and development economics, often using experimental and quasi-experimental methods.
