Welcome

I am a PhD candidate in Economics at the University of Sussex and a Doctoral Fellow at the International Centre for Tax and Development (ICTD).

About me

My research studies how tax systems and frontline bureaucrats shape state capacity, with a focus on Pakistan. My current work centres on subnational taxation, bureaucratic discretion, and the design of digital public infrastructure in property and excise taxation. I also work closely with government partners through IDEAS on teacher recruitment, merit-based reforms, and education policy.
