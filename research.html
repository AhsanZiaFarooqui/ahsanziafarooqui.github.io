Property tax, bureaucratic discretion, and effective progressivity in Punjab

PhD thesis project · with Excise & Taxation Department, Government of Punjab

Theoretical and empirical work on how the shift from Annual Rental Value to Capital Value taxation changes revenue, compliance, and the distribution of tax burdens when inspectors retain discretion over assessments and enforcement.

Tracking tax compliance: Evidence from Pakistan’s track-and-trace system

with collaborators at LUMS & ICTD

Administrative and firm-level evidence on how digital monitoring and invoicing affect VAT compliance, production, and firm behaviour in Pakistan’s excise and sales tax system.

Merit-based teacher recruitment and bureaucratic rules

with provincial education departments

Work on the political economy and implementation of merit-based recruitment rules for public-school teachers, combining policy timelines, protests data, and administrative records.
