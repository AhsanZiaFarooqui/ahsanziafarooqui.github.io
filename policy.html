Policy Work

Evidence-based collaborations with government, international organizations, and policy partners

With over seven years of experience working with governments and non-governmental organizations, I lead and contribute to impact evaluations, policy design, and advisory work. At IDEAS Pakistan, I lead the consulting wing, providing evidence-based impact evaluation to various international NGOs including UN bodies working in Pakistan, helping these organizations design studies and evaluate the impact of their programs through primary evidence collected via opinion-based surveys.

Taxation & Public Finance

Working with federal and provincial revenue authorities

Punjab Government

Property Tax Reform in Punjab

Excise & Taxation Department, Government of Punjab

Advisory work embedded within a legislatively mandated property tax reform. Contributions include analysis of the transition from Annual Rental Value to Capital Value taxation, design of digital self-assessment portals, and development of updated valuation schedules. Research conducted in close collaboration with the provincial government to inform progressive and equitable property tax policy.

Federal Board of Revenue

Digital Public Infrastructure for Tax Administration

With FBR, ICTD & IDS

Collaborative work with Pakistan's Federal Board of Revenue on the role of digital public infrastructure in modernizing tax administration. Focus areas include NADRA identity data integration, the FBR Maloomat taxpayer nudging portal, digital invoicing systems (Hubble), and the Track-and-Trace production monitoring system. Contributing to the evidence base for the Digital Nation Pakistan Bill.

ICTD · LoGRI

Subnational Public Finance and Equity

With ICTD and Local Government Revenue Initiative

Policy research on property tax utilisation, equity, and subnational public finance. Focus on how local governments can increase their own-source revenue while maintaining progressivity and public support. Research in collaboration with LoGRI on local government revenue mobilisation across developing countries.

Governance & Policing

Building citizen trust through evidence-based institutional reform

EGAP · Princeton

Community Policing Reform in Punjab

With IDEAS, LUMS & Princeton University

Co-investigator on a multi-country study (EGAP MetaKeta IV) aimed at increasing citizen trust in the state through innovative sub-national policing reforms. Oversaw the implementation of randomized community policing interventions across multiple police beats in Punjab, working with the Police Service of Pakistan. The research tested citizen-centric problem-oriented policing and alternative dispute resolution forums.

Gender · Policing

Women's Safety and Gender-Responsive Policing

With IDEAS & Police Service of Pakistan

Designed and evaluated community policing interventions that create separate spaces for women citizens while integrating male and female officers into joint teams. The program increased women's participation in community forums and facilitated reporting of gender-based violence in segregated settings.

Education & Human Development

Improving public service delivery through institutional design

Education

Teacher Recruitment and Merit-Based Reforms

With provincial education departments

Research and technical input on teacher recruitment and merit-based reforms with provincial education departments, including analysis of recruitment rules and protest data. Studies how bureaucratic rules interact with local political pressures in the education sector.

The Asia Foundation

COVID-19 Impact on Women in the Informal Economy

With The Asia Foundation & IDEAS Pakistan · 2020

Led a study exploring the multidimensional impacts of COVID-19 on women in Pakistan's informal economy, covering home-based workers, domestic services, salon workers, agriculture workers, teachers, and micro-enterprise owners. The research documented significant increases in domestic abuse and declines in employment and wages.
